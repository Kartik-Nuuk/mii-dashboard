🎯Mission

Deliver Nuuk-quality products from our India manufacturing network at target cost, with zero disruption to sales or operations through the BIS transition.

Near-term focus (6–12 months)

Stabilise SKD assembly across current partners · Complete BIS certification for pipeline · Hit China-equivalent NLC.

🔭Vision

Build a cost-advantaged, resilient India manufacturing base — driving deeper localisation, supply chain optimisation, and negative working capital via supplier credit leverage — while holding the Nuuk quality bar.

Long-term (18+ months)

Deep sub-assembly localisation · DPO > (Inventory + AR days) · SKD → CKD → full MII for flagship SKUs.

⚖️Guiding Principles

  1. 1Quality before cost, cost before speed.The China quality bar is the minimum for India. We do not trade quality for cost or speed.
  2. 2Process is the operator.SOPs, PFMEA, IQC, OQC, PDI drive outcomes. Heroics are failure signals.
  3. 3Lot is the unit of discipline.Every lot is a controlled cycle — defined inputs, gates, sign-offs. No lot starts or closes without its checks.
  4. 4Partners are co-owners, not vendors.Held to Nuuk's cost and quality bar through contracts, scorecards, and shared CAPA.
  5. 5Close the loop on failure.Every market return and warranty case feeds PFMEA, CAPA, and SOPs. Weekly review is the hinge.
  6. 6Capital efficiency is a KPI.Negative WC via supplier credit days > inventory + receivables days is an explicit design goal.
  7. 7Leaders' eyes on exceptions.Review cadences focus on variance and exceptions, not averages. Daily during production, weekly otherwise.
